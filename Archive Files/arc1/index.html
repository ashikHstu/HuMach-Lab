Welcome to HuMach Research Lab

At HuMach Research Lab, we are dedicated to advancing the frontiers of technology through cutting-edge research in Natural Language Processing, Biomedical Signal Processing, Medical Research, Computer Science, Generative AI, and Agentic AI. Our interdisciplinary team of experts collaborates on innovative solutions that bridge the gap between theoretical research and real-world applications, driving progress in healthcare, artificial intelligence, and beyond.

Our mission is to create impactful technologies that enhance human-machine interactions, improve medical diagnostics, and foster sustainable advancements in AI-driven research. Join us in exploring the limitless possibilities of human-centric machine learning.
