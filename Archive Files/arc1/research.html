Research Scopes

Natural Language Processing

Advancing algorithms for understanding and generating human language, enabling applications in chatbots, translation, sentiment analysis, and more.

Bio-Medical Signal Processing

Developing techniques to analyze biological signals for diagnostics, monitoring, and therapeutic applications in healthcare.

Medical Research

Conducting interdisciplinary research to improve medical practices, drug development, and patient outcomes through data-driven insights.

Computer Science Research

Exploring fundamental and applied computer science topics including algorithms, data structures, and software engineering methodologies.

Generative AI

Creating AI systems capable of generating new content, from images and text to complex data structures, revolutionizing creative and analytical processes.

Agentic AI

Developing autonomous AI agents that can make decisions, learn from environments, and perform complex tasks with minimal human intervention.
