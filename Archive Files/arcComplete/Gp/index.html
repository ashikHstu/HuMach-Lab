Where Human Intelligence Meets Machine Intelligence.

HuMach Research Lab conducts cutting-edge research in AI, healthcare, and intelligent systems to create impactful, human-aware technologies.

Human-Centered Machine Intelligence for Real-World Impact

The name "HuMach" comes from Human and Machine. HuMach Research Lab focuses on human-centered AI with applications in healthcare, Generative AI, Agentic AI, intelligent systems and other AI areas.

  • Human-Centered AI Design: Transparent and trustworthy AI aligned with human needs.
  • Healthcare & Biomedical AI: Intelligent analysis of biomedical and medical data.
  • Advanced Machine Intelligence: NLP, generative AI, and agentic AI research.

We promote interdisciplinary research and real-world impact. Our work bridges innovation and practical deployment, especially in healthcare.

Research Scopes

Check our Research Scopes

Machine Learning

Machine Learning develops algorithms that learn patterns from data to make accurate predictions and decisions. We work on supervised, unsupervised, and real-world data-driven learning models.

Biomedical Signal Processing

This area analyzes physiological signals such as EEG, ECG, and EMG to extract meaningful health information. Our research aims to improve diagnosis, monitoring, and clinical decision support systems.

NLP

NLP focuses on enabling machines to understand, interpret, and generate human language. Our research explores text analysis, sentiment understanding, and language-based intelligent systems.

Deep Learning

Deep Learning uses multi-layer neural networks to model complex patterns in large datasets. We focus on representation learning for vision, speech, and biomedical applications.

Generative AI

Generative AI creates new content such as text, images, audio, and synthetic data using advanced learning models. Our work emphasizes responsible generation and domain-specific applications.

Agentic AI

Agentic AI studies autonomous systems that can plan, reason, and take actions toward defined goals. We explore intelligent agents capable of decision-making, collaboration, and adaptability.
